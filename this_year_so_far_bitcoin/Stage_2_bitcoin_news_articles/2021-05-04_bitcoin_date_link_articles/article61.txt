Read why Joanna Ossinger says that Bitcoin’s dominance in cryptocurrency markets is steadily declining on Bloomberg Quint :

Bitcoin’s domination of total cryptocurrency market value is declining as its next-biggest rival Ether reaches the $3,000 milestone. The rise of Ether suggests there’s room for more than one winner among digital tokens as the sector evolves. Bitcoin now accounts for about 46% of total crypto market value, down from roughly 70% at the start of the year, and Ether makes up 15%, according to tracker, CoinGecko.
