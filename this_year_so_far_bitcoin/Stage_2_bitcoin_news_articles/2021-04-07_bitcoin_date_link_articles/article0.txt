Billionaire venture capitalist Peter Thiel is worried that China is using bitcoin to undermine the U.S.

In comments Tuesday during a virtual event for the Richard Nixon Foundation, and first reported by Bloomberg News, the co-founder of PayPal PYPL, +1.28% , Palantir Technologies PLTR, +1.34% and Founders Fund said bitcoin “threatens fiat money, but it especially threatens the U.S. dollar.”
