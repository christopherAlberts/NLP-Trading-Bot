Bitcoin surged early on Friday after Elon Musk tagged the cryptocurrency in his Twitter biography.

The price of bitcoin BTCUSD, +0.87% was last up 12.3% at $37,351, according to CoinDesk prices, after the Tesla TSLA, +1.28% chief executive appeared to express support for the cryptocurrency. After changing his Twitter TWTR, +0.55% bio to #bitcoin, he then tweeted “In retrospect, it was inevitable.”

Twitter Chief Executive Jack Dorsey — a vocal supporter of bitcoin — had the same bio as Musk early on Friday but it’s unclear when the platform’s founder made the change.
