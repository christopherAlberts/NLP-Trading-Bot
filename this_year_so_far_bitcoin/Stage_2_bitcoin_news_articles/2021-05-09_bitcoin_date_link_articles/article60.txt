Dubai, UAE; Dubai Airports added more choice and connectivity for its customers with the launch of Serene Air's inaugural flight from Lahore to Dubai on the afternoon of May 6. The airline will operate three flights a week on a 189-seater B737-800 aircraft between Lahore's Allama Iqbal International Airport and DXB Terminal 2. Pakistan emerged as the second most important country destination for Dubai in terms of passenger volumes in the first quarter of 2021 with nearly half a million passengers.
