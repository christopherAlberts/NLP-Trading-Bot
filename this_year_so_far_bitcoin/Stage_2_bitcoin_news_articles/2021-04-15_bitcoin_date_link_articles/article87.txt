XRP drops 9%, Bitcoin at $63,300 – crypto movers and shakers

Posted on April 15, 2021 by Jonathan Symcox share

Ripple’s XRP coin has dropped 9% in value in 24 hours, the biggest fall in the crypto world.

At the time of writing (7am), XRP was worth $1.76, still up an astonishing 88% on a week ago.

Other movers were WazirX (WRX) and DigiByte (DGB), both up 28% in the 24-hour period, while Stellar (XLM) and Tron (TRX) dropped 8.5% and 6.1% in 24 hours.

Bitcoin (BTC) is now worth $63,300, down 1.2% on 24 hours ago and up 10.6% on a week ago.

Ethereum (ETH) is valued at $2,475, up 4.5% (24 hours) and 22% (7 days).
