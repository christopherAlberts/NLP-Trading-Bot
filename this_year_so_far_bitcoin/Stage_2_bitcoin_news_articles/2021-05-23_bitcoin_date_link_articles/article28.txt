Bitcoin fell by 8.9 per cent to $34,156 at 1530 IST on Sunday, losing $3,344.54 from its previous close.

Bitcoin, the world's biggest and best-known cryptocurrency, is down 47.4 per cent from this year's peak of $64,895.22 on April 14.

Ether, the coin linked to the ethereum blockchain network, dropped 11.5 per cent to $2,031.96 on Sunday, down $264.35 from its previous close.
