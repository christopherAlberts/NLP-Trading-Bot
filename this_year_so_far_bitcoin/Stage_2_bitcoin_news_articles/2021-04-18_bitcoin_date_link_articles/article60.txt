Dogecoin’s astronomic rise continues, Bitcoin down to $56,000 – crypto movers and shakers

Posted on April 19, 2021 by Jonathan Symcox share

Dogecoin has almost quadrupled its value in a week.

At the time of writing (7am), DOGE – created as a joke – was worth 33 cents, up 360% on a week ago.

Among the other movers was NEO, which has almost doubled in price in seven days to $122.

Bitcoin (BTC) is now worth $56,600, down almost 6% on a week ago.

Ethereum (ETH) is valued at more than $2,245, up almost 5% in seven days.

Ripple (XRP) is worth $1.49, up 12% in seven days.
