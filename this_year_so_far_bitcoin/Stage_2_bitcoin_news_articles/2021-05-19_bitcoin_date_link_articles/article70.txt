Leading crypto plays outside the U.S. retreated as bitcoin BTCUSD, -0.12% and other cryptocurrencies plunged in value on Wednesday. In Canada, the big fallers were crypto exchange-traded products, with the 3iQ CoinShares Ether ETF Trust ETHQ, +4.92% falling 27% and the CI Galaxy Ethereum ETF ETHXB, +5.85% falling 25%. In London, Argo Blockchain ARB, +0.78% lost 19% and Online Blockchain OBC, fell 13%. The Swiss-listed CoinShares Physical Litecoin LITE, +4.59% dropped 50% and the 21Shares Ethereum ETP AETH, +1.24% fell 40%.
