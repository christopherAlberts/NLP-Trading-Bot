some characteristics of digital money could promote their adoption beyond the issuing jurisdiction the european central bank ecb has noted in a recent review of the international role of the euro specific design features can also incentivize nonresidents to use a digital euro as a means of payment unit of account and store of value the regulator sayssafety is a key feature the bank points out
